About Us

About JOROBEAN #1

JOROBEAN #1 is built for comfort, made for movement. Designed and printed on-demand, each pair represents the future of sustainable footwear. Our revolutionary 3D-printing process eliminates waste and delivers a product that's tailored just for you.

The concept for JOROBEAN came from a desire to challenge traditional footwear manufacturing methods. By utilizing cutting-edge 3D printing technology, we've created a shoe that requires no assembly, no glue, and no stitching. The result is a uniquely sustainable product with minimal environmental impact.

About the Designer

I'm an independent designer pushing the boundaries of footwear. Each pair is printed locally, supporting small-scale manufacturing while maintaining the highest quality standards. My mission is to combine innovation with sustainability, creating shoes that are as kind to the planet as they are to your feet.

With a background in industrial design and a passion for sustainable practices, I've spent the last five years refining the JOROBEAN concept. What began as an experiment has evolved into a revolutionary approach to footwear design that challenges industry norms.

Our Process

Every JOROBEAN #1 is created through a precise digital workflow:

  1. Each design is digitally modeled for optimal comfort and durability
  2. When you place an order, your shoe is sent directly to our local printing facility
  3. High-performance materials are used to create a single-piece construction
  4. The finished product requires minimal post-processing, reducing resource waste
  5. Your unique pair is delivered directly to you, eliminating excess inventory
